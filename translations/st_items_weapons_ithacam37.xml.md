```
One of most recognized pump-action shotguns of the United States owning the longest production run for a pump-action shotgun in history, the Ithaca M37 was designed for police, military and general civilian use. It utilizes a novel combination ejection and loading port on the bottom of the weapon, leaving the sides closed to the elements. Sporting a shortened barrel and pistol grip, the "Riot" model is widely considered the standard issue M37.\n \n
