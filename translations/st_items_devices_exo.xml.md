Scientific paper penned by R.R. Romanovich and A.N. Perchenov, detailing the strange effects that Flash artifacts have on electrically-powered machinery. When a Flash artifact is subjected to a positive electric current in vacuum, the artifact projects a variously sized anomalous field that vastly increases the physical output of electrical machinery in the area - motors run faster, CPUs overclock, and even mechanical watches move faster. Various experimental logs note the propensity of the machines to burn themselves out due to high heat and friction generated if the field is sustained for a prolonged period of time, and most of the paper is otherwise speculation at what could cause such a behavior. The latest prevailing theory is a quantum level influence increasing the flow of electricity and strength of magnetism. However, such theories are unconfirmed. In the middle of the paper is a diagram detailing the containment chamber construction of the artifact used in experimentation.\n\n
